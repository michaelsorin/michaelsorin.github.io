About me

My name is Michael Sorin and I'm a rising senior at Colgate University. I was recruited to school to play on the varsity men's soccer team but decided to leave the team my softmore year to have a broader college experienece and purue an entrepreneurial career.

InstaTute's vision is to be recognized as a global leader in learning innovation and technologies to help support the 21st century of learning and teaching. We are currently attempting to accomplish that by working with universities and students to create world-class, agile, peer tutoring programs and personalized digital calendars. My dream is to be a successful entrepreneur so that I can my platform and services to empower others around me.
